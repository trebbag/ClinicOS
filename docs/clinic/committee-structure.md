# Committee structure

## Leadership committee
- cadence: monthly
- outputs: strategic priorities, staffing decisions, high-risk escalations

## QAPI committee
- cadence: monthly
- outputs: quality review, incident themes, CAPA decisions, dashboard review

## HR / training committee
- cadence: monthly
- outputs: onboarding status, competency gaps, performance-review tracking

## Revenue / commercial committee
- cadence: monthly
- outputs: payer issues, service-line revenue review, pricing governance

## Service-line governance reviews
- cadence: monthly or quarterly depending on service
- outputs: role compliance, audit results, retraining decisions, public-claims review
